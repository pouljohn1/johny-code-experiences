Convex Chef is a **vibecoding tool** that helps you build web applications without writing any code. Think of it as having a skilled developer who builds exactly what you describe.

## The Magic of Vibecoding

Vibecoding means building software by describing your ideas in natural language. Instead of learning programming languages, you simply tell Convex Chef what you want:

> "Create a todo list app where I can add tasks, mark them as complete, and delete them."

That's it! Convex Chef understands your description and builds a working app for you.

## How Does It Work?

Convex Chef uses AI to understand your descriptions and automatically:

1. **Design the interface** - Creates buttons, forms, and layouts
2. **Build the backend** - Sets up data storage and logic
3. **Make it work** - Connects everything together

You focus on **what** you want. Chef handles **how** to build it.
