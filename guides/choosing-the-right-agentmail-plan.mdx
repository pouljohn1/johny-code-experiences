## Understanding AgentMail's Pricing Tiers

AgentMail offers five distinct plans, each designed for different stages of AI agent development and deployment. Here's a quick overview:

### Playground Plan (Free)

Perfect for getting started and testing the waters:

- **3 inboxes** for your AI agents
- **3,000 emails** (sent and received combined)
- **3GB storage** for email data
- **Cost**: Free

This plan is ideal for individual developers, hobbyists, or anyone evaluating AgentMail for proof-of-concept projects.

### Developer Plan ($20/month)

When you're ready to build actively:

- **10 inboxes**
- **10,000 emails** per month
- **10GB storage**
- **10 custom domains**
- Full API access, webhooks, and custom domain support
- **Cost**: $20/month

This tier suits developers and small teams working on active AI agent development.

### Starter Plan ($100/month)

For growing AI agent applications:

- **50 inboxes**
- **50,000 emails** per month
- **50GB storage**
- **50 custom domains**

- **Cost**: $100/month

Small to medium businesses and scaling AI projects will find this tier provides the headroom needed for growth.

### Enterprise Plan ($500/month)

## Calculating Your AI Agent's Requirements üìä

Before selecting a plan, you need to estimate three key metrics:

### Email Volume

Consider these factors:

1. **How many agents will you deploy?** Each agent may handle different email volumes.
2. **What's the agent's purpose?** Customer support agents send more emails than monitoring agents.
3. **Frequency of interactions**: Daily summaries versus real-time responses.

**Example calculation**: If you have 5 customer support agents that each send 100 emails and receive 50 emails per day, that's `5 √ó 150 √ó 30 = 22,500 emails/month`.

### Storage Requirements

Storage accumulates over time based on:

- Email body size (plain text vs. HTML)
- Attachment sizes (PDFs, images, documents)
- Retention period (AgentMail offers indefinite storage)

**Pro tip**: If your agents process attachments like invoices or reports, storage needs increase significantly. Plan for at least 2-3x your initial estimate to account for growth.

### Number of Inboxes

Each AI agent typically needs its own inbox for:

- Unique identity and authentication
- Separate workflows and purposes
- Easy monitoring and debugging

Don't forget to account for:

- Testing and staging environments (typically 20-30% of production)
- Future agent expansion plans
- Specialized agents for different departments or functions

## When to Upgrade Your Plan ‚¨ÜÔ∏è

### From Playground to Developer

Upgrade when you:

- Need more than 3 agents in production
- Exceed 3,000 emails in testing
- Want to use custom domains for professional appearance
- Require webhook integration for real-time processing

### From Developer to Starter

Consider upgrading if:

- You're deploying 10+ agents
- Monthly email volume approaches 8,000+ (80% of limit)
- You need multiple custom domains for different projects
- Storage is consistently above 8GB

### From Starter to Enterprise

Move to Enterprise when:

- You have 40+ active agents
- Email volume regularly exceeds 40,000/month
- You need priority support for business-critical operations
- Your team size grows beyond 10 people

### When to Request a Custom Plan

Custom plans make sense if you:

- Process over 250,000 emails monthly
- Require dedicated infrastructure for security or compliance
- Need white-label solutions for your clients
- Have specific data residency or regulatory requirements
- Want 24/7 dedicated support with SLAs

### For Growing Startups and Medium Teams

**Best choice: Starter Plan**

The Starter plan offers 5x the capacity of the Developer plan for 5x the price‚Äîlinear scaling that makes budgeting predictable. This tier provides enough runway for growth without constant plan changes.

**Key benefit**: With 50 inboxes and 50 custom domains, you can separate development, staging, and production environments while still having capacity for expansion.

**Best choice: Enterprise or Custom Plan**

- **Support needs**: Priority support reduces downtime and accelerates development
- **Infrastructure requirements**: If you need dedicated resources or white-label solutions, custom plans provide better value despite higher upfront costs

**ROI consideration**: Calculate the cost of building and maintaining your own email infrastructure versus using AgentMail. Most enterprises find that AgentMail's specialized AI-native features (structured data extraction, semantic search) save significant development time.

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid ‚ö†Ô∏è

### Underestimating Growth

Many developers start with the Playground plan and quickly hit limits. If you're planning to launch publicly, start with at least the Developer plan to avoid service interruptions.

### Ignoring Bi-directional Email Counts

Remember: both sent AND received emails count toward your limit. An agent that sends 100 emails and receives 100 responses uses 200 of your monthly allocation.

### Not Planning for Testing

Always reserve capacity for testing environments. A good rule of thumb: production should use no more than 60-70% of your plan's limits, leaving headroom for QA and development.

### Choosing Based on Price Alone

The cheapest plan isn't always the best value. Consider:

- Time saved with priority support
- Development velocity with adequate limits
- Peace of mind knowing you won't hit quotas during critical launches

## Making Your Decision üéØ

Here's a simple decision framework:

1. **Calculate your monthly email volume** (sent + received)
2. **Estimate number of agents** you'll deploy (including test/staging)
3. **Assess storage needs** based on attachment usage
4. **Add 30-50% buffer** for growth and unexpected spikes
5. **Compare your totals** against each tier's limits
6. **Factor in support needs** and infrastructure requirements
7. **Choose the plan** that gives you comfortable headroom
