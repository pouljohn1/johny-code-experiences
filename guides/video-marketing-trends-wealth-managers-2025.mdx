## Measuring ROI: What to track
Focus on metrics that connect to business outcomes:
- Engagement rate and average watch time
- Lead conversion rate from video viewers
- New client meetings and referral growth
- Retention signals (repeat views, follow-ups)

Tools to consider: Wistia, Vidyard, native YouTube Analytics, and your CRM for tracking conversions.

### Using analytics to refine content
- Identify top-performing topics and hooks.
- Adjust length and format based on average watch time.
- A/B test thumbnails, hooks, and CTAs while keeping all versions compliant.

## Common Pitfalls (and how to avoid them)
- Overproducing: polished videos are good, but perfect can delay consistency. Start simple and iterate.
- Ignoring compliance: always use approved scripts and archive final videos.
- One-size-fits-all content: tailor format and tone to each platform.
- No measurement: track a few KPIs from the start and review monthly.
